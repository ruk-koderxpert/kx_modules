# Approvals Teams

## Overview

The **Approvals Teams** module introduces flexible and configurable multi-level approval workflows in Odoo. It allows businesses to define custom approval teams, assign roles, and configure hierarchical approval structures for various models like sales orders, purchase orders, and invoices.

This module enhances control, accountability, and efficiency in business process management through clear approval responsibilities and tracking mechanisms.

---

## Key Features

- Create **Approval Teams** with designated team leaders and members.
- Configure **multi-level approval steps** for different business documents.
- Assign approvers based on **models** (Sales Order, Purchase Order, Invoice, etc.).
- Define **approval thresholds** based on monetary amounts.
- Easily track team members, approvals, and statuses through user-friendly **form and kanban views**.
- Set up **company-specific** or **model-specific** approval teams.

---

## Installation

1. Copy the module folder `approvals_teams` into your Odoo custom addons path.
2. Update the apps list.
3. Search for **Approvals Teams** in the apps menu.
4. Click **Install**.

---

## Usage

1. Go to **Approvals Teams > Teams** to define new approval teams.
2. Add team members, specify team leads, and assign applicable models.
3. Define approval rules, levels, and amount thresholds.
4. Integrate with your sales, purchase, or accounting processes to enforce the defined approval steps.

---

## Configuration

- Configure access rights under **Settings > Users & Companies > Users**.
- Define approval models and rules under the **Approvals Teams** menu.
